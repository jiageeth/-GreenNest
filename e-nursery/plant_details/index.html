About this Plant

The Fiddle Leaf Fig (Ficus lyrata) is one of the most popular indoor plants, known for its stunning architectural presence and large, glossy leaves. Native to western Africa, this tropical plant has become a favorite among interior designers and plant enthusiasts alike.

With proper care, your Fiddle Leaf Fig can grow up to 6-10 feet indoors, making it perfect for filling empty corners or serving as a dramatic focal point in any room.

Care Guide

Light: Bright, indirect light. Place near an east or west-facing window.

Water: Water when the top 2 inches of soil are dry. Typically once per week.

Humidity: Prefers 30-65% humidity. Mist leaves occasionally.

Temperature: Keep between 60-75°F (15-24°C).

Fertilizer: Feed monthly during spring and summer with diluted liquid fertilizer.
